====

agristoolkit is a stata that allow allow to compile long-format statistical table from complex statistical survey and extract statistical tables from it to be included in reports.

In this exemple we are going to use the nhanes2 dataset to long-format tables containg number of people with hight-blood pressures in the US by Region , Sex and Age group.

Twwo statistical indicators will be produced:  

**1. Pproportion of people with diabetites** (in percentage)  
**2. Proportion of people with high blood pressure** (in percentage)  
**3. Total number of  that have had an heart attack** (count)  


These two indicators will be desagregated by Region, Sex and Sex groups with will represent our estimation domains.


~~~~
<<dd_do>>
webuse nhanes2, clear

label def rur 1 "Urban" 2 "Rural"
label val rural rur

genmdt region sex rural , marginlabels("region@USA" "sex@both")  ///
mean(highbp diabetes) total(heartatk) integer(heartatk)          ///
units("highbp@%" "diabetes@%" "heartatk@people")                 ///
indicator(                                                       ///
"highbp@Pproportion of people with diabetites"                   ///
"diabetes@Proportion of people with high blood pressure"         ///
"heartatk@Total number of  that have had an heart attack"        ///
)
<</dd_do>>
